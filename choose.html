Why choose us

When you wish to receive a delivery or transport an item, you don't want any uncertainty. At the
same time, if you're a trucker, you don't want to have any doubts about your role, job security, or
flexibility. At Linestar logistics, we eliminate all of this by providing a tailored approach that
speaks to your needs.

Our fleet

To ensure rapid dispatching and reliable deliveries, we
regularly evolve our fleet on a 24 month basis. Every driver is
assigned to one of the latest Freightliner, Volvos, Kenworth, or
International vehicles before their commercial release. Using
new trucks and trailers enables us to limit major time
constraints like delays and breakdowns in transit.

Plus, we regularly conduct risk assessments and services to ensure that each vehicle runs
smoothly. BestPass and PrePass transponders are also attached to carriers, bypassing
significant time delays in weight stations.
