Since beginning

At Linestar Logistics, we wouldn't be where we are today if it wasn't for
the dedicated team that works night and day for us driving across the
country. We're proud to say that most of our drivers who have been with
us since the beginning are still with us today. With our founder originally
being a trucker himself, we know how important it is for a driver's rights
to be understood.

$1700-$2300 weekly

That's why, if you drive with us, you're treated like family, provided with job security,
a competitive salary, and the latest freight vehicles. To be more specific, our drivers
are earning between $1700-$2300 weekly. At the minimum, they're earning $1200
without any commission or bonuses. Most importantly, we pay you on time, cover
your layovers and detention too.

We prioritize our drivers

We also understand how tiring long haul driving can be. For this
reason, we prioritize our driver's comfort and safety by using the
latest Freightliners, Volvos, Kenworth, and International's well
before their commercial release.

24/7 specialist logistics team

We know first hand the risks, burdens, and time delays drivers can face, which is
why we equip our vehicles with Samsara devices, with forward facing cameras, E
logs, Transponders for tolls and scales, GPS, and more. Most importantly, we
have a 24/7 specialist logistics team who monitor your vehicle and are there to
help you with any problem or reload at any time you need.
